M6 complete. Implementation pushed as `1f5642f`.

Implemented:
- Final ray mode shades a 75%-per-axis warp target: 480x320 at 640x427, 960x540 in 1280x720 scenarios, 56.25% ray count.
- Exact separable offset-quadratic forward/inverse warp; finite-density sharp center, progressively softer edges.
- Ray pass writes Rgba16Float HDR color plus R32Float hit distance.
- Full-resolution unwarp bilinearly samples color, nearest-loads distance, and reconstructs WebGPU reversed-Z with `clip.z / clip.w`; sky writes 0.
- F9 non-Final modes bypass warp and remain full resolution.
- F11 displays the raw warp buffer; sun overlay is suppressed only for that Final-mode diagnostic.
- F12 remains downstream and captures the final result.
- Resize rebuilds warp textures and bindings from the internal render size.
